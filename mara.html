Experience Maasai Mara

The Maasai Mara is Kenya’s most iconic safari destination. It is world-famous for the Great Migration and unmatched predator sightings including lions, cheetahs, and leopards.

Wildlife Highlights

  • Big Five (lion, elephant, buffalo, rhino, leopard)
  • Great Wildebeest Migration
  • Hot air balloon safaris
  • Maasai cultural encounters

Why Visit

This is where Africa feels most alive — endless golden plains filled with wildlife action every day of the year.

Best Time to Visit

July – October (Migration season) but wildlife viewing is excellent year-round.
